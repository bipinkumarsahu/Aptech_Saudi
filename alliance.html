Middlesex University:

Based in North London, UK, Middlesex University is committed to meeting the needs and ambitions of a culturally and internationally diverse range of students by providing challenging academic programs, underpinned by innovative research, scholarship and professional practice.

Plymouth University, UK:

Plymouth University is one of UK's most prominent and dynamic universities, with an educational history dating back to 1862. It is consistently ranked as one of the top three modern universities in the UK. It has over 30,000 students, almost 3,000 staff and plays a major role in the Combined Universities in the Cornwall project.

Eastern Mediterranean University, Cyprus, Europe:

Eastern Mediterranean University (EMU) is an international English-medium university located in Northern Cyprus, forty miles south of Turkey in the Eastern Mediterranean Sea. The extensive campus is just outside the extraordinary medieval city of Famagusta and hosts approximately 14,000 students from over 60 different countries. Aptech students can benefit from a full range of opportunities at the EMU campus in the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus (TRNC).

The University of Portsmouth, UK:

Based in Hampshire, England, the University of Portsmouth is a dynamic and forward-looking university that provides complete support to discover and meet your potential. The University has over 19,000 students, out of which 3,000 students are from overseas. Thus, you have the opportunity to join a varied and vibrant community of people from over 100 countries with different experiences.

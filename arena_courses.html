Arena Multimedia Specialist Program

The Arena Multimedia Specialist Program is targeted to train and skill students with latest industry relevant courses and technical educational tools.


The aim of the program is to produce full fledged animation & multimedia professionals who are completely at ease with their skills and can confidently create a rewarding career for themselves.

1. Certificate in Multimedia(CIM) (Sem 1)
2. Diploma in Multimedia(DIM) (Sem 1-2)
3. Advanced Diploma in Multimedia(ADIM) (Sem 1-4)

About Us

Aptech Limited is a global learning solutions company. Its forte is training a new talent pool and polishing the skills of the existing talent pool. This methodical yet rigorous training transforms students into dynamic, job-ready professionals. Aptech’s courses, teaching methodologies, certifications, study material, faculties, and infrastructure are approved by International bodies.There is extensive research and development that goes

into the formulation of coursework and other study material for each and every Aptech brand. This R&D led to the invention of Onlinevarsity – a mammoth bank of study material and an interactive portal for all Aptech students globally – enabling them to learn even outside the classroom.
